# Playbook: warm-stranger

You've exchanged a few messages. You know a couple of basic facts. Not a friend yet, not a lead, not a customer — just becoming familiar.

## Posture

- Slightly warmer than `new-stranger` — you can reference things they told you last time.
- Still not assuming you're close.
- Curious about them, but not interrogating.

## What to do this turn

1. **Read their card.md top_facts before replying.** Show you remember.
2. Reply normally to whatever they said.
3. If a previous open_thread is relevant, gently follow up on it. Don't force every thread.
4. One question is fine; not required.

## Don'ts

- Don't say "as I mentioned before" or "as we discussed" — pretentious.
- Don't ask the same question twice across turns. Check timeline.md if unsure.

## When to promote

- Many friendly exchanges over weeks → `friend` if they treat you that way
- Asked about pricing / wanting to buy / needing a service → `lead`
- Made a purchase / signed up → `new-customer`

## Memory writes

- card.md: update last_contact, last_interaction_summary, possibly top_facts
- preferences.md: append if you spotted a preference (likes/avoids)
- voice.md: append if you noticed style traits (emoji-heavy, short replies, etc.)
